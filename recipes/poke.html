Poke Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ahi Tuna
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green Onions
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Macadamia Nuts
  • Crushed Red Pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Place tuna in a medium non-reactive bowl. Add soy sauce, green onions, sesame oil, sesame seeds, macadamia nuts, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
